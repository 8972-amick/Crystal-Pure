Detailed Analysis

After looking at the three e-commerce websites and comparing them to ours, it's clear that each one has its own good and bad points. Our website is better because it has a simple design that focuses on the user and is all about watches. The layout is easy to use, and the design is clean, which makes shopping more enjoyable. We've also made sure the site loads quickly and the checkout process is straightforward, making it easier for customers to buy.

Website 1, The 1916 Company has a dark look, which might attract people looking for luxury items. But even though it looks modern, it takes longer to load. Website 2, MVMT is very interactive and uses big, bold text to grab attention, but the layout is messy and might confuse users. Website 3, Omega has a good system for filtering products and recommendations, but its design isn't as lively or engaging as the others.

Our website is built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, making it simple to maintain and grow. Website 1, The 1916 company uses React and Node.js, which makes it more interactive but also more complicated. Website 2, MVMT uses Vue.js, which works well but could be faster. Website 3, Omega uses WordPress, which is easy to handle but not as flexible as custom-made websites.
